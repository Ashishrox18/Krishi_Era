# Viewing Offers and Counter Offers Guide

## For Buyers: Where to See Your Submitted Offers

### Step 1: Navigate to the Listing
1. Login as buyer (buyer@gmail.com)
2. Go to "Browse Farmer Listings" from the dashboard
3. Click on any listing you're interested in

### Step 2: Submit an Offer
1. Click "Submit Offer" button
2. Enter your offer price and quantity
3. Add an optional message
4. Click "Submit Offer"

### Step 3: View Your Offer
After submitting, the page will reload and show a blue section titled "Your Offer" with:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Your Offer                          [PENDING]   │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Your Offer Price: ₹400/kg                       │
│ Quantity: 50 kg                                 │
│ Total Amount: ₹20,000                           │
│                                                 │
│ Your message: "Interested in bulk purchase"    │
│                                                 │
│ [Update Offer]  [Award & Purchase]             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 4: View Farmer's Counter Offers
If the farmer sends a counter offer, you'll see an orange section below your offer:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Farmer Counter Offers:                          │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Counter Price: ₹450/kg                          │
│ "This is premium quality, worth the price"     │
│ March 7, 2026, 3:30 PM                          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Step 5: Respond to Counter Offers
You have two options:
1. **Update Offer** - Submit a new counter price
2. **Award & Purchase** - Accept the current terms and finalize the deal

## For Farmers: Where to See Received Offers

### Step 1: Navigate to Your Listing
1. Login as farmer (farmer@gmail.com)
2. Go to "My Listings" from the dashboard
3. Click on any listing that has offers

### Step 2: View Received Offers
Scroll down to the "Received Offers" section. You'll see all offers from buyers:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Received Offers (2)                             │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Buyer: Buyer User                  [PENDING]    │

│                                                 │
│ Offered Price: ₹400/kg                          │
│ Quantity: 50 kg                                 │
│ Total Amount: ₹20,000                           │
│                                                 │
│ Price Comparison:                               │
│ Your minimum price: ₹375/kg                     │
│ Buyer's offer: ₹400/kg ✓                        │

│ "Interested in bulk purchase"                   │
│                                                 │
```

### Step 3: Respond to Offers
You have two options:
1. **Counter Offer** - Propose a different price
2. **Accept Offer** - Accept the buyer's offer and finalize the deal

Click "Counter Offer" to open a modal:

## Offer Status Meanings

### For Buyers:
- **PENDING** - Waiting for farmer's response
- **COUNTERED** - Farmer sent a counter offer (check the orange section)
- **ACCEPTED** - Farmer accepted your offer! Deal is done 🎉
- **REJECTED** - Farmer declined your offer

### For Farmers:
- **PENDING** - New offer waiting for your response
- **COUNTERED** - You sent a counter offer, waiting for buyer's response
- **ACCEPTED** - You accepted the offer! Deal is done 🎉
- **REJECTED** - You declined the offer

## Quick Navigation

### As Buyer:
1. Dashboard → "Browse Farmer Listings"
2. Click on a listing
3. Submit offer
4. Return to the same listing page to see your offer and counter offers

### As Farmer:
1. Dashboard → "My Listings"
2. Click on a listing
3. Scroll to "Received Offers" section
4. View all offers and respond

## Tips

### For Buyers:
- Your offer is saved and visible when you return to the listing page
- You can update your offer anytime before it's accepted
- Counter offers from farmers appear in the orange "Farmer Counter Offers" section
- Once accepted, you can proceed to award the contract

### For Farmers:
- All offers appear in the "Received Offers" section of your listing
- You can see price comparisons with your minimum price
- Negotiation history shows all back-and-forth offers
- Accepting an offer automatically finalizes the deal

## Notifications

Both buyers and farmers receive notifications when:
- A new offer is submitted
- A counter offer is sent
- An offer is accepted
- A deal is finalized

Check the bell icon (🔔) in the top right corner to see all notifications.

## Example Workflow

### Complete Negotiation Flow:

1. **Buyer submits offer**: ₹400/kg for 50 kg
   - Buyer sees: "Your Offer" section with PENDING status
   - Farmer sees: New offer in "Received Offers" section

2. **Farmer counters**: ₹450/kg
   - Farmer sees: Offer status changed to COUNTERED
   - Buyer sees: Orange "Farmer Counter Offers" section appears

3. **Buyer updates offer**: ₹425/kg
   - Buyer sees: "Your Offer" updated with new price
   - Farmer sees: Negotiation history updated

4. **Farmer accepts**: ₹425/kg
   - Both see: Status changed to ACCEPTED
   - Deal is finalized automatically
   - Invoice generated (for procurement requests)

## Summary

- **Buyers**: View your offers on the listing detail page (blue "Your Offer" section)
- **Farmers**: View received offers on your listing detail page ("Received Offers" section)
- **Counter offers**: Appear in dedicated sections with clear visual indicators
- **Negotiation history**: Shows all back-and-forth offers in chronological order
- **Status badges**: Color-coded to show current offer status
- **One-click actions**: Accept or counter with simple buttons

The system is designed to make negotiation transparent and easy for both parties!
